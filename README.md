## 👋 About Me
Hey, I'm Danny =)

I build a **modern discord bot, web apps, dashboards, APIs and backend systems**.

I’m the creator of **PowerBot** — an all-in-one Discord bot with a modern dashboard available at **dash.pwr.lol**.

I also develop **dispatch and incident management systems** for **fire departments** and **emergency medical services** — both for real-life workflows and roleplay environments.

Currently, I’m also working on **Emergency Life Germany**, a Roblox emergency roleplay project with its own **dashboard**, **backend**, **API** and external **dispatch center**.
